DOUGHNUT INGREDIENTS MEASURMENT AND PROCEDURES:

INGREDIENTS FOR DONUT ARE;

  • FLOUR.
  • SUGAR.
  • BUTTER.
  • YEAST.
  • MILK (OPTIONAL).
  • EGG (OPTIONAL).
  • FLAVOR (OPTIONAL).

MEASURMENT FOR DOUGHNUT;

  • FOR FLOUR OF 1kg.
  • SUGAR > 140g.
  • BUTTER > 50g.
  • YEAST >20g.
  • EGG WHICH IS OPTIONAL > 2eggs.
  • MILK WHICH IS OPTIONAL > 2tea spoon full.

PROCEDURES FOR PREPARING YOUR DOUGHNUT;

STEPS ->
  1. ADD ALL DRY AGENTS TOGETHER AND MIX WELL. (Dey agents are those ingredients that are in powdered form).
  2. ADD YOUR BUTTER TO THE MIXTURE AND MIX PROPERLY.
  3. DO A SALT WATER SOLUTION. (This method of adding salt in water helps the salt to spread round your pastry rather than adding it direct to the dry agents).
  4. NOW ADD WATER TO YOUR MIXTURE AND MIX WELL. (Mix until the dough is smooth).
  5. Let it a little time to rise (10min).
  6. Mold your doughnut to the size of your choice (after molding let it for 5min to rise).
  7. Fry in a shallow vegetable oil of low heat.
